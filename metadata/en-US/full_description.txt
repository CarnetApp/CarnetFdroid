Carnet is more than an usual note taking app.




- Complete editor : bold/italic/underline/color/highlight
- Insert images / crop / review
- Floating text zones
- Open HTML format
- Organize with folders and choose root storage folder depending on your needs
- Re-open your last note quickly thanks to floating shortcut
- Transparency to take note from another app behind
- Quickly see your recent notes
- Search amount your notes
- Protect the app with a pin code (won't encrypt notes)
- Statistics : words/sentences/characters
- Sync with Google Drive
